Estimation and Prediction of Hospitalization and Medical Care Costs

Problem statement:

The problem statement for estimation insurance cost is to develop a model that can accurately predict the cost of insurance for a given individual or group of individuals. This is a challenging task, as there are many factors that can affect the cost of insurance, including age, health status, lifestyle choices, and occupation. The healthcare industry faces the challenge of accurately estimating and predicting medical costs for individuals. Accurate cost estimation and prediction are crucial for healthcare providers, insurance companies, and individuals to plan and manage healthcare expenses effectively. However, the complexity and variability of medical procedures, treatments, and healthcare services make it difficult to estimate and predict costs accurately.
